Design Principles

White space

Birkenstock

In the Birkenstock screenshot we can see white space and clean design applied. Our focal point are the Arizona sandals and all around it is a clean space that allows us to percibe better the product design. This helps to create a better experience for the user.

Visual Hierarchy

Apple

I feel Apple site is a good example of visual hierarchy, the first thing we notice are the iPhones picture, after that we see the product name and then we are directed to the call to action "know more" or "buy". These steps allow the user to reach the conversions on the site.

Contrast

Librerías Gandhi

Gandhi is a mexican bookstore, its brand colors are yellow and purple and these create a big contrast in its publications. We can notice the contrast between the background and typography colors and between the other elements like the book and illustrations.
